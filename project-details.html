Project Overview

The GLP-1 therapeutics market represents one of the fastest-growing segments in healthcare, with significant implications for patient outcomes and provider practices. This project focused on developing and executing a comprehensive market expansion strategy to increase provider adoption of innovative GLP-1 treatments.

Working closely with cross-functional teams, I led the market analysis efforts, identified key opportunity areas, and developed targeted engagement strategies that resulted in measurable adoption increases across our target territories.

The Challenge

  • Market Complexity

    Rapidly evolving competitive landscape with multiple new entrants and shifting payer dynamics.

  • Provider Education Gap

    Limited understanding among providers of optimal patient selection and treatment protocols.

  • Access Barriers

    Complex prior authorization requirements and varying coverage across different payer types.

Strategic Approach

Market Intelligence Gathering

Conducted comprehensive analysis of market dynamics, competitive positioning, and provider sentiment across target territories.

Segmentation & Targeting

Developed provider segmentation model to identify high-potential prescribers and prioritize engagement efforts.

Education Program Development

Created tailored clinical education materials and training programs addressing key knowledge gaps.

Go-to-Market Execution

Launched multi-channel engagement campaign with coordinated sales, medical, and market access activities.

Results & Impact

Provider Adoption

Achieved 40% increase in provider adoption rates within 6 months of program launch.

Patient Reach

Expanded access to 12 million patients through improved provider network coverage.

Education Impact

95% of trained providers reported improved confidence in patient selection.

Speed to Market

Delivered full program launch in 6 months, 2 months ahead of original timeline.
